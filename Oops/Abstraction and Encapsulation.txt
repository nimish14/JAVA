Encapsulation:
Wrapping up of data members and methods in a class is knows as encapsulation.
It solves the implementation level issue. How we are writing code, what all classes, objects are being used to achieve that abstraction.

AAbstraction:
Hiding unecessary details and showing only valuable information.
We are using println to print something, how it is printing or working internally we do not care.   
Design issue. How it is being actually designed. It solves the design level issue
